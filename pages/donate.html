Why Donate:

Cal State LA is proud to be one of the fourteen colleges and universities selected nation-wide to participate in the first annual NASA Swarmathon robotics competition. Our participation in this national competition is a great honor for our campus, and weâ€™re seeking your help in supporting our effort.

While NASA is providing the majority of the components necessary to work on the project, participants are not provided with any means to attend the actual physical competition to be hosted on April 20th and 21st, 2016 at the Kennedy Space Center in Florida. For a team of twenty students, the total cost for travel adds up to about $20,000. We are reaching out to you in hopes of receiving your financial support to help us make this trip possible. Any amount you are willing to contribute will help towards our goal of having all students attending the competition. This will be an incredible opportunity for the students to interact with students from other universities, meet with NASA engineers, and visit the Kennedy Space Center, a renowned place of engineering accomplishments. We hope to gain your support for this excellent opportunity!

The Cal State LA Swarm Robotics team operates under the University, therefore financial support is tax deductible and will be managed by University Auxiliary Services. Please make checks payable to University Auxiliary Service (Cal State LA Swarm Robotics). To find out more or donate online please visit csulaswarm.org and direct any questions you might have to donate@csulaswarm.org. If you wish to donate via phone or speak to someone in person feel free to contact Lily Nguyen, Director of Development 323-353-3043. We hope to hear from you soon.
